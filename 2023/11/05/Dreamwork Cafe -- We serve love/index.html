Dreamwork Cafe -- We serve love

Author: Ivan Li Date: November 5, 2023  0:00:00

This is actually a tiny voluntary activity that was organized by some of my friends and me. The initial intent was straightforward: This is a cafe run by patients of Down’s Syndrome. And it is opened right next to where I lived.

Simple furnishing, simple coffee options, this cafe is destined to have average sales. However, our team wanted to explore the uniqueness of this cafe. We prepared interviews for every barista, trying to make a video recording this experience.

Our first trouble came when we were forced to think of other ways of interviewing because they couldn’t understand our questions that weren’t carefully expressed. We started to realize how condescending we were being, how our intent to help them, as Immanuel Kant put it, was nothing but hypothetical imperatives.

We started to make changes.

And here was what we achieved.

In fact, the Chinese version was more watched, because we focused on Chinese social media more. This cafe is in Shanghai nevertheless.
